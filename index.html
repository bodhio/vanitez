philosophy

A vanitas is a symbolic work of art showing the transience of life, the futility of pleasure, and the certainty of death. In today's society we shy away from the concept death and avoid talking about our own mortality, yet embracing it can be quite liberating.

Maranasati means mindfulness of death, and is a Buddhist meditation practice that may use visualization to meditate on the nature of death. A similar concept in the Western world is 'memento mori'. The Vanitez collection uses Van Gogh's satirical vanitas painting as a visual reference to invoke maranasati and memento mori.

process

The artworks are created through relief printmaking techniques, namely linocut - in which negative space is carved out to create the design. Multiple designs are carved by hand to create a frame by frame animation. Lush patterns, textures, and colour palettes are added to create a unique piece that embraces a particular vibe.

project

Vanitez is not a typical NFT project, but it does incorporate aspects that are important to collectors.

rarity

Each NFT is hand crafted and non-generative frame by frame gif. The textures, patterns, colour palettes, and description are all unique to the individual artwork. Every 10th drop from the collection is a longer animation.

roadmap

This project is organic and fluid, so there is no formalized and rigid roadmap. Much like how the universe is in constant flux of change - the roadmap is malleable and dynamic based on the needs of the Vanitez community. Join the discord to contribute and influence the trajectory of the project.

utility

This is first and foremost an art project, so there is intrinsic value in the art itself. That being said, there are perks for owning a Vanitez piece. This includes; exclusive airdrops, private discord channels, and a direct voice to help shape the future of the project.
